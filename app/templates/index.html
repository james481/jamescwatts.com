About Me

I'm a software architect who lives with my wife and three cats near Salem, OR. While not writing code I enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, backpacking, climbing (rock / ice / snow), building things and watching hockey (Go Avs!).

I work as a Software Architect and Consultant on a variety of projects remotely from my home office.

I specialize in web application architecture and development, mostly in PHP (Symfony, Drupal, Magento, Zend, NIH) and jS (jQuery, React). I also dabble in other languages (Elixir, Python, C++, etc), microcontroller programming and other electronics projects.
