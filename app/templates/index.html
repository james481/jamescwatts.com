About Me

I'm a Software Developer who lives with my wife and two cats near Providence, RI. While not writing code I enjoy outdoor activites like hiking, backpacking, climbing (rock / ice / snow), video games and watching hockey (Go Avs!).

I work for VisionLink as a Software Engineer, and am also Chief Technology Officer of PrimeWellness, both in Boulder, CO. I work remotely the majority of the time, and spend a few days a month in Boulder.

I specialize in web application architecture and development, mostly in PHP (Symfony, Phalcon, Zend, NIH) and jS (jQuery, React). I also dabble in other languages (python, C, etc) from time to time.
